About

I'm an artist, writer, coder, general tinkerer, and Software Engineer at Microsoft Docs, previously the development team lead at Yesler. Based in Seattle Washington. I've had the opportunity to work on a number of great projects and my work focuses on bringing design and development together to build functional and visually interesting web experiences. I started developing as a hobby in the early 2000's and have been developing professionally for the past 10 years. I am not code exclusive but tend to focus on the front-end, JavaScript, Typescript, Node, CSS/SASS, build, and dev process.
